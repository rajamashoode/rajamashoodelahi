About Me

Hello! I'm Raja Mashood Elahi.

Based in the scenic city of Abbottabad, Pakistan, I am a dedicated and results-oriented Social Media Marketer with a passion for helping businesses thrive in the digital landscape. My journey in this dynamic field began in 2019, driven by a fascination with the power of social platforms to connect people and build brands.

My Mission

My mission is to empower businesses, both local and international, by crafting compelling social media strategies that not only enhance online presence but also foster genuine engagement and drive measurable growth. I believe in the power of authentic storytelling and data-driven insights to achieve impactful results.

My Journey & Experience

Since starting in 2019, I've actively sought opportunities to learn and grow. Through multiple internships between 2022 and 2023, I gained invaluable hands-on experience. Currently, I'm applying my expertise as the Social Media Marketing Manager for Briana J Credit Repair, a US-based company in South Carolina, managing their online presence remotely. This role has further honed my skills in cross-cultural communication and managing campaigns for diverse audiences.

Certifications & Skills

Continuous learning is key in digital marketing. I hold professional certifications from industry leaders: Google's "Foundations of Digital Marketing and E-commerce" and Meta's "Social Media Marketing Professional Certificate". My skill set includes strategy development, content creation, platform management (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.), paid advertising, community engagement, and performance analytics.
